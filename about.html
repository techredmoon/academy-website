Shourya Academy - Mission

The school was conceived with the motive of bringing public school education to the door step of the common man and sending a maximum number of its cadets to National Defence Academy. The School is indeed rendering a yeoman service to the nation by providing quality education to the childrens. It enables boys and girls to take up an illustrious career as officer in the Armed Forces of the Nation and other celebrated professions.

The school emphasizes on all-round development of the cadets so that they consistently strive hard to live up to the expectations of their parents and nation at large.

The objectives of our school are as follows.

1. To give education to all irrespective of caste, creed and colour in an atmosphere of purity, efficiency, and discipline with special stress on character building.

2. To import value based education through cultural activities, co-curricular activities, games and sports.

3. To activate academic excellence through a team of dedicated teachers.

4. To develop integrity and character in children.

5. To create awareness among students to maintain environmental purity and ecological harmony.

6. To develop qualities of a good human being like respect for the parents and elders, honesty, truthfulness etc.

7. To provide to the country a future generation in excellent qualities of character.
